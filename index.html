Key Features

Footprint-style panel

Per-bar volume and delta visualized in a footprint-like panel below the chart. Separate rows for Volume, Delta, Bid, Ask and multiple CVDs with per-day normalization.

Advanced heatmaps

Separate heatmap modes for Volume, Bid, Ask, Delta and CVD values, with positive/negative color schemes matching the footprint idea of buying vs. selling pressure.

Alert engine

Volume spikes are detected based on configurable lookback and volume ratio. Alerts can trigger sound, bar coloring, column highlight in the price chart and optional message box.
